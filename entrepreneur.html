Earthy Creations (click me!)

I founded Earthy Creations, an startup that sells upcycled art made by students. Our online platform and art shows incentive students to make and sell upcycled art! The point is to make sustainability fun and give a creative hobby more purpose, so students continue to practice both creativity and sustainability throughout college. We now have 40+ student artists at Duke University, and we are projected to sell $15k in student art by the end of the year!
